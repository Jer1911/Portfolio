Details

We work with multiple cloud providers and operating systems, and are experts in AWS, Terraform, Kubernetes, Docker, Ansible, Chef, and Linux/Unix-based systems. Our custom solutions build the infrastructure programmatically from the ground up, strictly segregating private and public subnets within the VPC and establishing bastion hosts as a single, hardened point of ingress from the internet. We fully automate the deployment of any kind of web servers and database layers a customer may need, and also secure front-ends with SSL-protected load balancers. All of our deployments utilize high-availability clusters and auto-scaling groups to ensure that your infrastructure can grow alongside your business and is never unavailable. We consider security the foundation of our practice, and accordingly, we provide intensive OS hardening, highly granular IAM policies, strict security group rules, dynamic network access control lists, and automated vulnerability scanning and reporting for all systems that we deploy. For customers requiring next-level security, we also provide penetration testing services and can build infrastructures specialized for such.
